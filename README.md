🌐 Campus Connect – Student Freelance Marketplace
Overview:
Campus Connect is a web-based platform designed to connect college students with freelance micro-jobs and projects posted by individuals, startups, or campus clubs. It empowers students to gain real-world experience, earn income, and build their portfolios while studying.

🧩 Problem Statement:
Many students have valuable technical and creative skills but lack opportunities to apply them in real-world scenarios. Simultaneously, clients often struggle to find affordable and skilled talent for short-term or small-scale tasks. Campus Connect bridges this gap by creating a centralized, skill-driven freelance ecosystem within college communities.

🔧 Key Features:
✅ Client Dashboard: Allows users to post job requests such as graphic design, video editing, data entry, content writing, and web development.

✅ Student Application Portal: Students can browse listings, apply to tasks based on their skill set, and communicate with clients.

✅ Secure Payment System: Integration of a basic transaction flow between clients and students upon task completion (can be scaled with Stripe or Razorpay).

✅ Task Management Panel: Track progress, manage deadlines, and upload deliverables from within the platform.

✅ User Profiles & Ratings: Both clients and students have profile dashboards with rating and review systems to build credibility.

🛠️ Tech Stack:
Frontend: React.js

Backend: Node.js, Express.js

Database: Firebase (Authentication & Realtime Database or Firestore)

Version Control: Git, GitHub
